RACE DAY INFO

This page contains all of the information you should need for race day.

Please also download the Race Pack [link] with race maps and emergency contact information.

Further information is on our FAQ and Facebook page.

This race consists of 3 distances (race maps below):

Ultra-Marathon (60km)

Marathon (42.2km)

Half-Marathon (21.1km)

The meeting point, registration, and race briefing for all distances is at Carlow Rowing Club, Carlow Town.

The Ultra-Marathon will have a slight route change this year.

The race starts in Carlow Town Park and proceeds along the banks of the River Barrow for 60km.

This will take runners to the beautiful and unspoiled St Mullins with a short loop back to Graiguenamanagh. We feel this is one of the most stunning parts of the route; you should enjoy the out and back immensely.

There will be 6 aid stations/checkpoints.

The Barrow Valley Marathon route starts in Carlow Town Park and meanders pleasantly along the River Barrow to finish 42.2km away in Graiguenamanagh. There are 4 aid stations/checkpoints along the route.
